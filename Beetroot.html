Steps to Growing Organic Beetroot:


1. Prepare the Soil: Amend well-drained soil with compost. Aim for a pH between 6.0 and 7.0.
2. Sow Seeds: Plant seeds ½ inch deep and 2-4 inches apart. Thin seedlings later.
3. Water Regularly: Keep the soil consistently moist, especially during early growth.
4. Weed and Mulch: Remove weeds and apply mulch to retain moisture.
5. Fertilize Sparingly: Use compost tea or a balanced organic fertilizer if needed. Avoid excess nitrogen.
6. Control Pests/Diseases: Use organic methods if pests or diseases appear.
7. Harvest: Pull beets when roots are 2-3 inches in diameter. Enjoy the greens too!
